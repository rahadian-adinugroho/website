Hey, I'm Rahadian — a backend engineer at Traveloka. I've been meaning to start a blog for a while now, and somehow it always got pushed to the backlog. Well, here it is finally.

This is where I'll write about things I think about at work and outside of it: distributed systems, API design, databases, and occasionally the messier human side of software engineering — like how teams make technical decisions, or what makes a codebase actually maintainable over time. Nothing too formal; just thinking out loud.

If something here is useful to you, great. If you disagree with something, even better — I'm always happy to be wrong in an interesting direction. Welcome aboard.
